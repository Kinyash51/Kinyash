About Me

A little more about me

Kenya, faith, curiosity, details, music, and the choice to keep moving.

I'm Trevor. I'm from Kenya.

I've never been the type to wait until I know exactly how something works before I start. I jump in, figure things out as I go, fix what breaks, learn what I need to learn, and keep moving. Most of the things that matter in my life started that way.

See more See less

I pay attention to details. The little things people overlook are usually the first things I notice. Whether it's a conversation, a place, a piece of writing, or something someone creates, I appreciate things that are thoughtful and made with intention.

I don't fit neatly into one box. Faith, creativity, curiosity, learning, and meaningful work all overlap for me. They're not separate interests — they're different parts of how I see the world.

I'm a perfectionist, but not the kind that never finishes anything. I care about getting things right. If something can be better, I'll probably keep thinking about it until it is.

There's almost always music playing somewhere in the background.

Right now, I'm in a season of growth. Learning new things. Taking chances. Making mistakes. Trying again. Choosing "why not?" more often than "maybe later."

I don't have everything figured out, but I'm moving forward, and that counts for a lot.

True things about me

  • There is almost always music playing.
  • I notice details most people miss.
  • Faith isn't part of my life — it's the foundation of it.
  • My first response to new opportunities is usually, "Why not?"
  • I believe growth comes from starting before you feel ready.
